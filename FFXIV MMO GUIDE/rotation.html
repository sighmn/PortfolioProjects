FAQ

What is the best tank?

Well, there is no "best tank", but a good starter tank is the warrior! Each tank has its own unique charm and should be considered.

Why do people call WAR a healer?

Warrior has an ability called bloodwhetting, and it is potent. The ability allows you to heal while doing damage, which, combined with your AOE abilities, gives you basically no damage taken for 8 seconds.

What is the most complex tank?

All the tanks are straightforward in how they are played. They just take a combination of reading the tool-tips and practice!
